Data Deletion Request

GCI Living General Trading LLC respects your privacy. If you would like to request deletion of your personal data collected through our Facebook Messenger integration, please follow the instructions below.

What Data We Collect

When you send a message to our GCIliving Facebook Page, we may store your Facebook User ID, the content of your message, and the timestamp of your message. This data is stored in our internal CRM system for customer follow-up purposes only and is never shared with third parties.

How to Request Data Deletion

Email: chrischen@globalcare-info.com
Company: GCI Living General Trading LLC
Location: Dubai, UAE

Please include your name, Facebook username or approximate date you sent a message, and a description of the data you want deleted. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data Retention Policy

We retain customer inquiry data for up to 2 years. Upon a valid deletion request, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.
